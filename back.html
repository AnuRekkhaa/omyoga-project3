IMPORTANT AASANAS

Paschimottanasana

Paschimottanasana, or Seated Forward Bend, is a calming and restorative yoga pose that stretches the entire back body. This asana promotes flexibility, relaxation, and a sense of inner peace.

Position:Hinge at the hips and slowly fold forward, reaching toward your feet while keeping your back straight.

Supta Matsyendrasana

Supta Matsyendrasana, or Reclined Spinal Twist, is a gentle twisting pose that promotes relaxation and helps release tension in the spine and hips. Named after the sage Matsyendra, this asana encourages flexibility and inner calm.

Position:Exhale as you lower your knees to one side, maintaining contact with the ground, while turning your head in the opposite direction.

Eka Pada Rajakapotasana

Eka Pada Rajakapotasana, or One-Legged King Pigeon Pose, is a deep hip opener that promotes flexibility and strength while providing a powerful stretch to the hip flexors and thighs. This pose encourages emotional release and enhances overall stability.

Position:Inhale as you lift your right leg and bring your right knee forward towards your right wrist, placing your foot near your left hip.
