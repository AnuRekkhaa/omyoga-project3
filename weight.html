IMPORTANT AASANAS

Virabhadrasana

Virabhadrasana Warrior Pose, is a powerful standing asana that symbolizes strength, courage,Enhances focus and concentration, opens the hips and chest, Boosts confidence and reduces stress and resilience.

Position:Bend your right knee over your right ankle, ensuring it aligns with the ankle and extend your arms parallel to the floor, palms facing down.

Trikonasana

Trikonasana, or Triangle Pose, is a foundational yoga asana that promotes balance, flexibility, and strength. This pose resembles a triangle shape and is excellent for enhancing overall body awareness and alignment.

Position:Hinge at the hip, reaching your right hand toward the right foot while extending the left arm upward.

Sarvangasana

Sarvangasana, or Shoulder Stand, is a foundational inversion pose in yoga that promotes physical strength and mental clarity. Known as the "mother of asanas," this pose offers a wide array of benefits for the body and mind.

Position:Support your lower back with your hands as you extend your legs straight up towards the ceiling.
