Popular Styles of Online Yoga are

1.Hatha: Focuses on physical postures (asanas), breath control (pranayama), and meditation to promote It promotes balance and flexibility, making it ideal for beginners.
2.Vinyasa: A dynamic and flowing practice that links breath with movement. Classes often vary in intensity and pace, creating a rhythm that can be both energizing and meditative.
3.Ashtanga: A structured and rigorous style that follows a set sequence of postures. It builds strength and stamina through a challenging sequence, focusing on breath and movement synchronization.
4.Yin: A slow-paced style that targets deep connective tissues and promotes relaxation. Poses are held for longer periods, encouraging introspection and mindfulness.
5.Restorative: A gentle practice that uses props to support the body in restful poses. It encourages deep relaxation and recovery, perfect for stress relief.

Whether you’re looking for a quick session or a deep dive into a specific style, there are plenty of resources available to help you on your yoga journey.

Health values

Yoga is the journey of the self, through the self,
to the self!

Yoga embodies a holistic approach to health, offering numerous benefits that enhance both physical and mental well-being. Through a combination of postures, breath control, and mindfulness practices, yoga promotes improved flexibility, strength, and balance. It effectively reduces stress and anxiety, fostering emotional stability and resilience. Additionally, yoga supports better posture and alleviates chronic pain, contributing to overall comfort and mobility.

Practicing yoga enhances respiratory function and boosts immunity, promoting cardiovascular health and overall vitality. It encourages mindfulness, which can aid in weight management and improve sleep quality. By fostering a strong mind-body connection, yoga not only nurtures individual health but also cultivates a sense of community and personal growth, making it a valuable practice for holistic well-being.

YOGA THERAPY

Yoga therapy is a holistic approach that combines traditional yoga practices with therapeutic techniques to promote physical and mental well-being.

STEP-1

Conduct an initial evaluation to understand the individual's physical, emotional, and mental health needs.

STEP-2

Create a customized yoga practice that includes yogasanas (postures), breathwork, and meditation.

STEP-3

Encourage the integration of yoga practices into daily routines, promoting long-term benefits.
