ABOUT US

Who we are?

OM YOGA is a non-profit organisation founded in 1986 by Dr.A.N. SHINDHIR SINGH (Ph.d in yoga).Our mission is to create a supportive and inclusive community where individuals can explore the transformative power of yoga from the comfort of their own homes. It aims to promote physical health, mental well-being, and community connection through virtual classes and resources. By leveraging technology, online yoga seeks to inspire individuals to cultivate mindfulness, balance, and personal growth in their lives. Our vision is to create a global community where individuals can practice yoga anytime and anywhere, fostering wellness and connection. It aims to empower people to embrace a holistic lifestyle, promoting physical health, mental clarity, and emotional resilience through accessible and inclusive yoga practices.

Here, we offer a diverse range of classes tailored to all levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners. Our experienced instructors are passionate about guiding you on your journey, helping you cultivate strength, flexibility, and inner peace. Whether you're looking to deepen your practice, reduce stress, or enhance your overall wellness, we have something for everyone. From invigorating flows to calming meditations, our sessions are designed to fit your lifestyle. Enjoy the flexibility of live sessions and on-demand content, all crafted to help you achieve balance, strength, and mindfulness. Start your journey to a healthier lifestyle today! Join us for live-streamed sessions, on-demand classes, and specialized workshops designed to meet your unique needs. Embrace the convenience of online yoga while connecting with a vibrant community of like-minded individuals. Together, we will inspire each other to grow, learn, and thrive.

Benefits

Our Assets

Improved Flexibility: Regular practice increases flexibility and range of motion in the joints.

Enhanced Strength: Many yoga poses build muscle strength, promoting overall body stability.

Stress Reduction: Yoga encourages relaxation and helps reduce stress and anxiety levels through mindfulness and breathing techniques.

Better Posture: Yoga promotes awareness of body alignment, leading to improved posture and reduced back pain.

Increased Focus and Concentration: The practice of meditation and mindfulness enhances mental clarity and focus.

Emotional Well-being: Yoga fosters emotional balance, helping to reduce symptoms of depression and promote a positive outlook.

ONLINE TRAINING

Online yoga training offers a flexible and accessible way to practice yoga from the comfort of your home. Through live sessions and on-demand classes, participants can explore various styles and levels, guided by experienced instructors. Each session emphasizes proper alignment, mindfulness, and personal growth, catering to individual goals and needs.

With a focus on building strength, flexibility, and mental clarity, online yoga training fosters a supportive community while allowing practitioners to develop a consistent practice at their own pace.
