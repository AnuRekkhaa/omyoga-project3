Mindfulness Meditation

Meditation is a powerful tool for managing stress, anxiety, and improving overall well-being. Practicing mindfulness and focusing on the present moment can lead to a more balanced and peaceful life.

The practice centers on observing thoughts and feelings without attachment or aversion, promoting a sense of calm and clarity, and concentrating on their breath.

Zen Meditation

A seated meditation that emphasizes breath and posture, often practiced in silence.

Zen meditation has been practiced for over 2,000 years and is considered one of the most sacred forms of meditation. Some popular Zen meditation styles include Tenzin-gyan, Jyotish, and Karma-gyan.

Loving-Kindness Meditation

Loving-Kindness Meditation or Metta, is a practice aimed at cultivating an attitude of love and compassion towards oneself and others. This meditation encourages positive feelings and connections, promoting emotional well-being and reducing negative emotions.

Participants often center their attention on the heart area, fostering warmth and compassion, starts with self-love and expands outward to all beings.

Duration: 4 days/week
